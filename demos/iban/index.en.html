IBAN form-filling with Yivi

This page describes how a visitor can easily disclose his/her IBAN bank account with Yivi. In particular, a form is filled-in automatically with reliable IBAN data.

In order to be able to try out the button below you need to have the Yivi app installed.

IBAN
BIC
Fullname

A few remarks:

  • This demo fills in the above bank account fields, but does not store or collect any data. As soon as the demo is closed, all information disappears.
  • This automatic form-filling with Yivi also works for other fields, like name, email address, phone number etc. This is much easier than typing the information by hand and prevents mistakes. Moreover, the website knows the data is reliable: it can see the source of the data, and on that basis decide its level of confidence.

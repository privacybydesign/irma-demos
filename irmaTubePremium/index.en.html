Become an IRMATube Premium member

This demo is not a real video-streaming service but it does demonstrate several aspects of IRMA. When you become IRMATube “premium member” you receive a membership card in your IRMA app with your name on it. Subsequently, you can use the data on this card to watch (trailers of) movies on the IRMATube main page. As a premium member you also have access to the premium contents when clicking the "Show premium contents" button.

In order to be able to try out yourself the buttons below one needs to have the IRMA app installed.

   

A few remarks:

  • No information about email addresses is retained or collected via this webpage. The revealed email addresses are only used for the purpose of this demo and disappear at the moment that the demo is closed.
  • In oder to become a premium member you need to disclose your name. Under the hood several chained IRMA sesssions are used. In the first sesssion your name gets disclosed which will be used without further user interaction in the second session. The second session will issue the premium membership card.

Demo Attestatio de Vita for your insurance

Via this page you can, simply and with costs, produce evidence of "being alive". Such evidence is required on a regular basis for certain insurance products.

Reveal personal data

Via the button below you will be asked to reveal via your IRMA app the following data: your family name, initials, data of birth. It will be checked whether they are at most 14 days old.

Background

  • You can reveal the requested data via the IRMA app on your mobile phone.
  • You need to have already loaded the relevant personal data in your IRMA app. In The Netherlands these data can be obtained online from government registers via municipalities.
  • These so-called BRP data can be loaded (or refreshed) into your IRMA app via this link.
  • After this demo your data will be removed immediately.

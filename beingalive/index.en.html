Prove that you are alive with Yivi

Via this page you can, simply and without costs, produce evidence of "being alive". Certain (live)insurances ask regularly for such a Attestatio de Vita.

Reveal personal data

Via the button below you will be asked to reveal via your Yivi app the following data: your family name, initials and data of birth. It will be checked whether they are at most 14 days old, that is, that they have been loaded into your Yivi app less than 14 days ago.

Background

  • You can reveal the requested data via the Yivi app on your mobile phone.
  • You need to have already loaded the relevant personal data in your Yivi app. In the Netherlands these data can be obtained online from government registers via municipalities.
  • These so-called BRP data can be loaded (or refreshed) into your Yivi app via this link.
  • After this demo your data will be removed immediately.
